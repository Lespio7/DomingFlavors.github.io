How to make a Pizza Margherita 🍕

Ingredients (1 medium pizza):

Dough:

  • 200g flour (1 + ½ cups)
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 120ml warm water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Topping:

  • 3–4 tbsp tomato sauce (fresh or canned, blended)
  • 100–150g fresh mozzarella (sliced or torn)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • olive oil for drizzling
  • Optional: pinch of salt & black pepper

Steps:

  1. Make the dough

    • Mix flour, yeast, and salt.
    • Add warm water + olive oil, knead until smooth (~5–7 min).
    • Cover and let rise 30–45 min (or until doubled).

  2. Prepare the base

    • Roll or stretch the dough into a thin circle (or rectangle).
    • Place on a baking tray or pizza stone.

  3. Add sauce & toppings

    • Spread tomato sauce evenly, leaving a 1–2 cm border.
    • Tear mozzarella into pieces and place on top.
    • Add fresh basil leaves.
    • Drizzle a little olive oil on top.

  4. Bake

    • Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F) if possible. Higher temp = crispy crust.
    • Bake 8–12 minutes (edges golden, cheese bubbly).

  5. Finishing touch

    • Optional: add chili flakes, oregano, or a little parmesan.
    • Slice and serve hot, like a pro pizza place 😎

Serve hot and enjoy the perfect mix of gooey mozzarella and fresh basil 🍕✨
